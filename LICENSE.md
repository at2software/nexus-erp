Definition of Tenant
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tenant means a distinct organization, company, or legal entity using
a deployed instance of NEXUS for its own business operations.

Each production deployment of NEXUS serving a separate organization
counts as one Tenant, regardless of the infrastructure used.

If multiple organizations use the same NEXUS deployment while operating
as separate businesses, each organization shall be counted as a separate
Tenant.

Development, staging, or testing deployments used internally by the
licensee do not count as Tenants.
