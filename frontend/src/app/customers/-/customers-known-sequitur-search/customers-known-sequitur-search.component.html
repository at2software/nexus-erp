what is known sequitur ?

Known Sequitur is your comprehensive call management interface for handling customer interactions while on the phone. It provides everything you need during a call: add new projects and leads, update existing project information, capture additional contact details and employees, view important customer statistics, and identify missing information (like birthdays or lead sources) so you can ask the caller directly.

getting started
  • use the search field on the left to find and open an existing contact
  • once selected, you'll be taken to the customer's tracking dashboard
phone client integration

connect your phone client to automatically open known sequitur when receiving calls :

{{getKnownSeqUrl()}}{{ '{' }}phoneNumber{{ '}' }}
Replace {{ '{' }}phoneNumber{{ '}' }} with the actual caller's phone number. This will automatically look up the customer or allow you to create/link a new one.
